House Of Words Library-About Us


Welcome to the “House of Words Library”! In a time of rapid change, we continue to stand at the center of intellectual life in our city.

With almost 4 million titles – both digital and physical – we're the largest academic library collection in the country. We provide a rich and diverse collection of resources, including rare books and electronic publications and databases to further the teaching and research programs offered at any university.

We're a progressive, modern library and we genuinely care about our readers.Our Academic Liaison Librarians, Peer Learning Advisors and other expert staff look after our community's needs in research, education and wellbeing.
